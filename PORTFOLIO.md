## Overview

This AI chatbot provides instant, accurate customer support by combining retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) with a polished, embeddable widget. It's designed for SMBs who need enterprise-grade support automation without enterprise complexity.

The chatbot learns from your existing documentation—FAQs, help articles, product guides—and provides contextual answers that feel human. When it can't help, it seamlessly escalates to your support team with full conversation context.

## The Challenge

Small and medium businesses face a brutal support equation:
- Customer expectations are set by Amazon and Apple (instant, accurate, 24/7)
- SMB budgets can't support round-the-clock support teams
- Hiring and training support staff is expensive and slow
- The same questions get asked hundreds of times

The result? Support teams drowning in repetitive tickets, customers waiting hours for simple answers, and business owners watching support costs eat into margins.

## The Solution

This chatbot tackles the problem at multiple levels:

**For Customers:**
- Instant responses to common questions
- Natural language understanding (no keyword matching)
- Multi-language support without separate implementations
- Smooth handoff when human help is needed

**For Support Teams:**
- 40-60% fewer tickets hitting the queue
- Context-rich escalations (no "what's your order number?" loops)
- Analytics on what customers actually ask

**For Business Owners:**
- 5-minute installation (single script tag)
- No code changes required
- Admin dashboard for knowledge base management
- Pay for value, not headcount

## Technical Highlights

- **RAG Architecture:** Combines vector similarity search with LLM generation for accurate, grounded responses
- **Embeddable Widget:** Single-line installation, works on any website
- **Knowledge Ingestion:** Automatically processes PDFs, help articles, and documentation
- **Conversation Memory:** Maintains context across multi-turn conversations
- **Admin Dashboard:** Non-technical users can manage knowledge base, view analytics, customize appearance
- **Bilingual Support:** Native English/Spanish without translation latency
